## Overview

Comparative analysis of Large Language Models (LLMs) and Small Language Models (SLMs) in the context of generative AI.
Explanation of different capabilities to customize and fine-tune models and limitations of deployment.

## Key Topics

- Architecture differences between LLMs and SLMs
- Capabilities for customization and fine-tuning
- Deployment considerations and resource requirements
- Real-world applications and use case selection
